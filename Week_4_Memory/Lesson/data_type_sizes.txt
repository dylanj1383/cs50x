char: 1 byte. Used to store a single character.
int: Typically 2 or 4 bytes. Used to store whole numbers.
float: 4 bytes. Used to store single-precision floating-point numbers.
double: 8 bytes. Used to store double-precision floating-point numbers.
short: 2 bytes. Used to store smaller integers.
long: Typically 4 or 8 bytes. Used to store larger integers.
long double: 10 or 16 bytes. Used to store high-precision floating-point numbers.
bool: 1 byte. Used to store boolean values.
